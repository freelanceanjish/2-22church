Who We Are

Our Story

Unpopular conclusions. Firmly grounded in Scripture.

We are hungry for the truth, even if that places us among the minority. Even if it comes down to one. If there is anything more beloved to us than the Word of God, it is a speck in the eye that will not allow us to see.

After fifteen years following Christ across different denominations, listening to international theologians, teachers, and writers, researching Scripture through historical references, creeds, and primary sources, and analysing events both past and present, we arrived at a conclusion we did not go looking for.

As someone once said: truth is stranger than fiction.
Indeed it is.

The time is at hand. Let us turn our hearts to the truth and come out of that which Scripture calls Babylon.

The Journey

Fifteen years across denominations.

We have sat in evangelical churches, charismatic services, reformed congregations, and independent fellowships. We have listened to sermons from well-known voices and unknown teachers in small rooms. We have read the creeds, the confessions, the systematic theologies, and the apologetics.

What we found consistently was a gap. Between what the apostles wrote and what was being taught. Between what Scripture says and what tradition had settled on. Between the questions people were actually carrying and the answers the institution was equipped to give.

Acts 17:11

The Bereans received the message with great eagerness and examined the Scriptures every day to see if what Paul said was true.

These were not comfortable questions. They led to uncomfortable places. We did not choose to arrive at the conclusions listed below. We followed the evidence where it led.

What We Found

Progressive conclusions from Scripture.

Each one took years of research, cross-referencing primary sources, and testing against the apostolic text. We hold them not as a creed to be defended, but as conclusions to be examined. Click any heading to explore the evidence.

1
One God, Eternal Father, Almighty, Most High.

Not a Trinity of co-equal persons, but the God of Abraham, Isaac and Jacob, the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ. Every apostolic epistle declares this from its very first line.

2
Jesus Christ is the Son of God.

Known in the New Testament as Lord, Messiah, King, Mighty God, High Priest, and the Second Adam. Distinct from the Father who sent Him, yet fully His.

3
The earth is not a spinning globe.

Scripture describes a circular earth founded upon pillars, enclosed by a firmament upon which the heavenly waters rest. This is the consistent, plain testimony of the biblical text from Genesis to Revelation.

4
The Tabernacle is circular, not rectangular.

The design God gave Moses embeds the constant Pi (3.14). It is the shadow of the actual heavenly dwelling, not a random architectural brief.

5
We are in the Short Season, not the Church Age.

The millennial reign of Yeshua on this earth ended around 250 years ago. Colonisation and world wars were attempts to destroy what once stood in glory all over the world. What history calls the Dark Ages is the disguise of the millennial age.

6
Secular Israel is not the true Israel.

Not by geography, biology, or biblical explanation. The mainstream Christian community's unconditional support for the modern secular state of Israel is, by scriptural standards, unfounded.

7
Current world events follow a deliberate script.

Written through media, religion, politics, and education progressively from the 17th century onward. It has been predicted more accurately by scientists and political theorists than by the church.

8
The 66-book canon is incomplete.

Books referenced within Scripture itself have been systematically excluded. Their exclusion serves a purpose. Their inclusion changes the picture considerably.

9
Western missionary Christianity is not the apostolic faith.

Shaped by colonisation, political agendas, the prosperity economy, and institutional interests. It does not meet the biblical standard on numerous counts.

Why We Share This

Not to argue. To invite.

We share these conclusions because we know there are many individuals who have reached similar places privately and have not found others willing to say plainly what they have come to believe. If you have arrived at any of these conclusions and are looking for people who take the question seriously, you are in the right place.

Search the Scriptures for yourself. Test everything. Hold fast to what is true.

Open Tables

No Platform

Honest Questions

No question is off limits. If the truth cannot survive examination it is not worth holding.

Come as you are.
