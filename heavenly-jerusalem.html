Heavenly Jerusalem

Why the 1,500-mile cube fails scale and pattern — and what dome geometry proposes from John's measuring reed.

Traditional FailsScriptureProposalGalleryArithmetic3D VideoSources

At the end of all things, the Bride is not a warehouse. She is a dwelling — curved like the tent Isaiah said God spreads over the circle of the earth.

Section I

Why the cube model fails

Prophecy charts universally depict New Jerusalem as a 1,500-mile steel cube — length, breadth, and height equal at twelve thousand furlongs. That is the traditional mainstream design. It fails both scale and pattern.

Traditional — fails

Cubic city (1,500 mi³)

3.375 billion cubic miles — larger than many moons, yet descending to earth.

Contextual — holds

Dome with circular footprint

2,800 mi diameter, ~14,000 mi apex, pi in the base area — tent scaled to glory.

  • Scale absurdity — A 1,500-mile cube cannot descend to earth as John sees (Revelation 21:10). The cubic reading imports modern zoning logic into apocalyptic measure.
  • Breaks architectural gospel — Ark, tabernacle, temple all curve. Only tradition's city goes flat at the climax.
  • Ignores "foursquare" context — Length equal to breadth describes a circular base as readily as a square one when read with Isaiah 40:22's circle.
  • Separates wall measure — 144-cubit wall (12²) is measured separately (Rev 21:17), suggesting height in v.16 is not simple box edge.
  • No soul in a warehouse — The Lamb and the Father are the temple (Rev 21:22). A dome dwelling fits throne-above-firmament; a cube fits industrial storage.
Section II

What John measured

Revelation 21:15–17 (KJV)
He measured the city with the reed, twelve thousand furlongs. The length and the breadth and the height of it are equal. He measured the wall thereof, an hundred and forty and four cubits.

Also: 4 Ezra 10:55–59 (Zion restored in bridal glory); Isaiah 40:22 (heavens as tent over the circle).

Section III

Dome-like bridal city: base diameter 2,800 miles, apex ~14,000 miles, footprint area 1,960,000π sq mi. Context from Project Arc.

Section V

Arithmetic

Cube reading (fails)

12,000 furlongs ÷ 8 = 1,500 mi/edge. Volume = 3.375×10⁹ cu mi.

Dome reading

d = 2,800 mi, r = 1,400 mi. Area = π × 1,400² = 1,960,000π sq mi. Apex ≈ 14,000 mi. Wall = 144 = 12² cubits.
