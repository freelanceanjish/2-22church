Heavenly Jerusalem

The final structure in Scripture's architectural sequence — read as dome, not cube.

I doubted the shoe-box temples in my heart long before I wrote a word. At the end of Revelation, why would the Bride descend as the one shape the Creator never plants in the field?

Section I
Before the Text

The inherited cube

Modern prophecy charts draw New Jerusalem as a steel-grey cube 1,500 miles per side — a city the size of a small moon. That reading hangs on the word "foursquare" and the assumption that length, breadth, and height must mean a box.

Mainstream assumption

12,000 furlongs per edge = cube. Flat faces, right angles, city as warehouse. Volume ≈ 3.4 billion cubic miles.

Contextual reading

Length equal to breadth = circular base. Equal height = dome ascending to throne. Radiant cap, not cold box. Footprint area in pi-square form.

Scripture has described sacred space as curved from Genesis onward. The final city is not where the pattern breaks. It is where the pattern completes.

Wall measured separately: 144 cubits = 12². Twelve gates, twelve foundations — the city's signature integer is duodecimal symmetry, the same family as tabernacle loops and tribal order.

Section III
Contextual Geometry

Dome, not cube

When length equals breadth on a level measurement, both may describe diameter. When height equals the same reed-length, the third dimension may describe apex altitude of a dome — the tent Isaiah 40:22 describes, scaled to the measure of God.

God's throne sits above the firmament throughout Scripture (Ezekiel 1:26; Isaiah 66:1). A domed city is not decorative speculation. It is where the architectural grammar has been pointing since the ark.

Section IV
Arithmetic

Furlongs and miles

Cubic reading (comparison)

12,000 furlongs ÷ 8 = 1,500 miles per edge.

Volume = 1,500³ = 3.375 billion cubic miles — difficult to reconcile with a city descending to earth.

Contextual dome reading

Base diameter: 2,800 miles. Radius r = 1,400 miles.

Footprint area = π × r² = π × 1,400² = 1,960,000π square miles (area in pi-square form).

Dome apex height: 14,000 miles — height-to-radius ratio 10:1, evoking tent pole ascent under the firmament curtain.

Wall and gate integers

Wall: 144 cubits = 12 × 12. Gates × foundations = 12 × 12 = 144.

√144 = 12. The city counts by dozens, not by secular zoning blocks.
