Find a Gathering

We are still forming, a small circle of friends who found Yeshua outside the walls of institutional Christianity.

We are still forming rather than running a congregation with an address, a roster, or a schedule. The burden behind that call lives on Our Story.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them."

Matthew 18:20
Where We Are

No fixed address. No building.

We do not own a building, hold a Sunday service, or keep a membership list. We meet as circumstances allow, in homes, around tables, in the open air, wherever people are comfortable gathering with their families and asking honest questions together.

The apostles did the same: Paul taught publicly and house to house, the early church broke bread in ordinary rooms. We are simply trying to follow their example.

◆
Who We Are Looking For

People who are searching.

We are looking for people who love the Word of God more than they love a religious system, people willing to test what they have been taught against what the apostles actually wrote, people who are not afraid of where the search leads.

You do not need to share all my conclusions. You only need to be serious about finding the truth rather than defending a tradition.

Open Tables

Food, Scripture, questions, meals are the oldest fellowship the church has ever known.

Honest Questions

No question is off limits here. If the truth cannot survive examination, it is not worth holding.

No Platform

No pastor, no stage, no production; everyone contributes, no one performs, the pattern of 1 Corinthians 14:26.

Prayer Together

Simply and directly, in the name of Yeshua, no scripts, no professional voices, just people before the living God.

Get in touch.

Use the Connect form and we will reply personally. We would genuinely love to hear from you, wherever you are in your search.
