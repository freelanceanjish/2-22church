Find a Gathering

We are forming. Friends who have found Yeshua outside the walls of institutional Christianity.

We are forming. Not yet a congregation with an address, a roster, or a schedule. Something more honest than that: a small number of friends who have been touched by the saving love of God and found Yeshua our Messiah outside the walls of institutional Christianity.

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them."

Matthew 18:20
Where We Are

No fixed address. No building.

We do not own a building, hold a Sunday service, or collect a membership list. We meet as circumstances allow, in homes, around tables, in open air, wherever people are comfortable gathering with their families and asking honest questions together.

The apostles did the same. Paul taught publicly and house to house. The early church broke bread in ordinary rooms. We are simply trying to do what they did.

◆
Who We Are Looking For

People who are searching.

We are looking for people who love the Word of God more than they love a religious system. People willing to test what they have been taught against what the apostles actually wrote. People who are not afraid of where that search leads.

You do not need to have arrived at the same conclusions we have. You need only be serious about finding the truth rather than defending a tradition.

Open Tables

We share food, Scripture, and questions. Meals are the oldest form of fellowship the church has ever known.

Honest Questions

No question is off limits. If the truth cannot survive examination it is not worth holding.

No Platform

No pastor. No stage. No production. Everyone contributes. No one performs. That is the pattern of 1 Corinthians 14:26.

Prayer Together

Simply, directly, in the name of Yeshua. Without scripts or professional voices. Just people before the living God.
